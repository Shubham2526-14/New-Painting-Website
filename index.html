Our Story

Where Art Finds
New Life

Founded in 2014, ARC Resources and Services is a fully-equipped private studio providing highly skilled and efficient services in art conservation. ARC — Art Restoration and Conservation — cares for an extensive range of art objects including paintings, works on paper, sculpture, miniature paintings, prints, written media and contemporary materials.

Our team of dedicated conservators is committed to fine art restoration with emphasis on preventive care and minimal intervention.
